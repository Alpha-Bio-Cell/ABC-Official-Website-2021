WHY ABC ?

  Alpha Bio Cell is a community of skilled and enthusiastic people working together to have their thinking caps on. This is a platform where people get to uncloak their skills, nurture them and put themselves on the line to success. We believe that our world is built on biology and our interdisciplinary understanding is the key to bring out the hidden technology in it. This is a place where you get to know the untapped potential that entrepreneurship ventures could offer. We behold biology not just as a science but a domain which has the potential to harbour various engineering principles from biochemical and computer science to mechanical and electrical. This is an abode of learning where we pinpoint all disciplinary elements under one roof to radiate our conception from ideation to execution.
  A little planning goes a long way to build a firm foundation. We are a team building doors to knock the opportunity that you wish to tackle, a place for you to learn, mastering new skills and chance to grow together for coming-of-age. Alpha Bio Cell is an initiative to nurture leadership so that entrepreneurs would not only lead in the scientific arena but also in the corporate world.

GUIDELINES

These are the General Guidelines to adhere to for Recruitment process-
  • The applications for recruitment will be circulated via google forms. Candidates are requested to fill the form before 28th July, 4 p.m.
  • Only one response per applicant is allowed.
  • The recruitment procedure consists of 2 rounds-
    • (i) Round 1- Candidates will be assessed on the basis of their aptitude and competency in the selected domain. Applicants will have to submit the task specified under the domain selected while filling out the application.
    • (ii) Round 2- Candidates clearing round 1 will be asked to appear for a Personal Interview, where the individual's interests and goals will be assessed for the club's culture and mission.
  • For Round 1, Candidates will be given two tasks out of which one will be mandatory along with a descriptive question related to the domain selected Round 1 tasks for the applicants will be mailed individually to the registered email. The task is to be completed within a two days timeline from 29th July - 30th July. The completed task should be submitted in the given google form in zipped format. Results for Round 1 will be announced tentatively by 31st July 11 A.M.
  • Round 2 Personal Interview will be conducted for the selected candidates on the same day from 3 P.M onwards.
  • All the registered applicants are requested to follow the Instagram Page of Alpha Bio Cell for the result related and further updates.
  • Tasks submitted by the individual should be authentic. Any form of plagiarism will be strictly considered for the selection procedure.
  • The details filled in by the applicants should be submitted correctly as per the instructions provided.
  • The responses of the candidates to the tasks listed should be orderly and clean-cut.
  • The completed task file should be uploaded in the proposed format.
  • A candidate has to appear in all the phases of the recruitment process to be considered for the selection. The candidates are advised to visit ABC Instagram Page regularly.
  • Candidates should strictly adhere to the specified deadlines. Selected candidates are expected to appear for a personal interview at the scheduled time.
